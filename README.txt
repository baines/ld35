					       : The Bloodening        2016 Alex Baines


Seek out the fabled room of blood within the deep dungeon.

Use your ability to *shapeshift* into a vampire bat to help you through
the trecherous journey.

Controls:

WASD / Arrows: Move
Space        : Turn into a bat

Pick up floating blood droplets to stay in bat form longer.
